What is Joint Genesis?

Joint Genesis is a dietary supplement from BioDynamix marketed to support joint comfort and mobility. It's taken daily in capsule form and is positioned toward older adults who want to stay active. Important: This is a dietary supplement, not a treatment for arthritis or any medical condition. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease, and individual results vary.
