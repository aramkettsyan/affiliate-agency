If your dog is smart but a little mischievous, the idea behind Brain Training for Dogs is that mental stimulation — not just obedience drills — solves many behavior problems. Here's an honest review of this popular course.

What is Brain Training for Dogs?

Created by certified dog trainer Adrienne Farricelli, it's an online course built around 21 games designed to boost a dog's intelligence and improve behavior, plus clear instructions for obedience commands. It's delivered digitally with lifetime access.

Who is it best for?

Dog owners who want a positive-reinforcement, force-free approach and are dealing with everyday issues like barking, chewing, pulling, or not listening. It works for most breeds and ages.

The good

  • Created by a credentialed professional trainer.
  • Force-free, game-based method that's enjoyable for owner and dog.
  • 60-day money-back guarantee through ClickBank.
  • Lifetime access and a members' area.

Things to keep in mind

  • Results require consistent practice — it's not instant.
  • Serious aggression issues warrant an in-person professional trainer.
  • Self-paced, so you supply the discipline.

The bottom line

Brain Training for Dogs is a well-regarded, positive-reinforcement course that's a great fit for owners who want to mentally engage their dog while fixing common behaviors. With a 60-day guarantee, it's low-risk to try. See the official page below.
