For boat-building enthusiasts and hobbyists, MyBoatPlans is one of the largest plan collections on the market. Here's a straight look at what you get and whether it's worth it.

What is MyBoatPlans?

It's a digital library of 518 boat plans plus boat-building guides and videos, covering everything from small dinghies and canoes to larger builds. Everything downloads instantly.

Who is it best for?

DIY boat builders and woodworking hobbyists who want a big reference of plans to choose from rather than sourcing individual designs.

The good

  • Huge variety — 518 plans across many boat types.
  • Includes build guides and videos, not just diagrams.
  • 60-day money-back guarantee through ClickBank.
  • Instant digital access.

Things to keep in mind

  • Digital downloads, not printed plans.
  • Boat building is a serious project requiring tools, space, and time.
  • Upsells appear after purchase.

The bottom line
