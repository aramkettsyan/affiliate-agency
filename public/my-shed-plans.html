Planning to build a shed, gazebo, or backyard structure and want plans you can actually follow? My Shed Plans (also known as Ryan Shed Plans) is one of the most popular DIY plan libraries online. Here's an honest look at what it offers and who it suits.

What is My Shed Plans?

It's a large digital collection of 12,000+ shed and woodworking plans, each with detailed blueprints, material lists, and step-by-step instructions. You download everything instantly, so you can start planning the same day.

Who is it best for?

DIYers and weekend builders who want a ready-made blueprint instead of designing from scratch. The huge variety means you'll find plans for small garden sheds up to larger workshops and outbuildings.

The good

  • Massive plan library covering sheds, gazebos, and outdoor projects.
  • Clear blueprints with cut lists and measurements.
  • 60-day money-back guarantee through ClickBank.
  • Instant digital access.

Things to keep in mind

  • Digital downloads, not a printed book.
  • Some upsells (extra plan packs) appear after checkout.
  • You'll need basic tools and a bit of building experience for larger projects.

The bottom line

If you want a deep, affordable library of shed and woodworking plans, My Shed Plans delivers on variety and clear instructions. The 60-day guarantee makes it low-risk to try. Check current pricing and the full plan list on the official page below.
