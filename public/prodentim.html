What is ProDentim?

ProDentim is a chewable probiotic supplement marketed for supporting the health of teeth and gums. The idea behind it is that the mouth, like the gut, has a microbiome, and the product aims to support a healthier balance of bacteria. It comes as a soft tablet you chew daily.

Important: ProDentim is a dietary supplement, not a medicine or a replacement for brushing, flossing, or seeing a dentist. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease, and results will vary from person to person.

Who might consider it?

People who already maintain good oral hygiene and are curious about adding a probiotic to their routine. If you're dealing with a specific dental problem, the right first step is a dentist — not a supplement.
