Secrets to Dog Training (from Kingdom of Pets) has been a staple in the online dog-training world for years. Here's an honest look at what it covers and who it's for.

What is Secrets to Dog Training?

It's a comprehensive digital dog-training program covering obedience, behavior problems, and understanding why dogs act the way they do. It's been one of the longest-running dog-training products available, delivered digitally.

Who is it best for?

Owners who want a thorough, all-in-one reference for common behavior problems — barking, digging, chewing, aggression basics, and obedience — rather than a single-issue guide.

The good

  • Broad coverage of obedience and behavior issues.
  • Established, long-running program.
  • 60-day money-back guarantee through ClickBank.
  • Instant digital access.

Things to keep in mind

  • Consistency is required to see results.
  • Severe aggression should involve an in-person professional.
  • Some material may feel general if you only have one specific issue.

The bottom line

Secrets to Dog Training is a solid, comprehensive choice for owners wanting a single reference for everyday training and behavior. The 60-day guarantee makes it low-risk. See the official page below.
