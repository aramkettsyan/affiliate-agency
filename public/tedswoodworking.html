TedsWoodworking Review

Check TedsWoodworking →

If you've ever wanted to build your own furniture, garden shed, or weekend woodworking project but didn't know where to start, you've probably run into the same problem most beginners do: finding clear, complete plans. TedsWoodworking is one of the longest-running products in this space, and in this review we'll look at what it actually is, who it's a good fit for, and where it falls short.

What is TedsWoodworking?

TedsWoodworking is a large digital library of woodworking plans — marketed as 16,000+ projects ranging from simple birdhouses and bookshelves to full furniture sets, sheds, and outdoor structures. Each plan is delivered as a downloadable document with diagrams, cut lists, and step-by-step instructions. It is a digital product, so you get instant access rather than a printed book.

Who is it best for?

It's aimed squarely at hobbyists and DIYers who would rather follow a proven blueprint than design a project from scratch. If you have a basic set of tools and want a deep catalog of projects to work through, the sheer volume here is the main appeal. Complete beginners benefit most from the step-by-step diagrams.

The good

  • Huge volume of plans — you're unlikely to run out of projects.
  • Beginner-friendly diagrams with cut lists and measurements.
  • Evergreen, well-established product — it has been a top seller for over a decade.
  • 60-day money-back guarantee through ClickBank, so you can try it with low risk.

Things to keep in mind

  • It's a digital download, not a physical book — you'll want a device or printouts in the workshop.
  • There are upsells presented after purchase (extra plan packs, software).
  • Quality varies across such a large library; the value is in the breadth.

The bottom line

TedsWoodworking is a solid pick if you want a large, affordable library of woodworking plans and you value quantity and step-by-step guidance. Because it's backed by a 60-day money-back guarantee, it's low-risk to try and see whether the plans match the kind of projects you want to build.
